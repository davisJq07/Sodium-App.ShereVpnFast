# 🚀 Sodium App - Share VPN Fast

A completely free, ad-free application designed for local internet sharing on your Android device—**superior to standard proxy apps**. 

This tool enables seamless VPN and proxy connection sharing via multiple protocols including **SOCKS5**, **HTTP**, and **Shadowsocks (SS)** with a single tap, completely **without requiring root access**.

---

## ✨ Features
- **100% Free & Open**: No subscriptions, no hidden fees.
- **Zero Ads**: Clean and uninterrupted user experience.
- **No Root Required**: Works safely on all standard Android devices.
- **Local & Secure**: Operates entirely offline on your local network with zero tracking.
- **Multi-Protocol Support**: Easily switch between HTTP and SOCKS5/SS proxies.

## 🛠️ How to Share Your VPN Connection

The application offers two flexible sharing methods. You can use them simultaneously or choose the one that best fits your workflow:

### 1️⃣ Method One: HTTP Configuration
1. Enable the **Hotspot** on **Phone A** (the host) and open the Sodium app, then tap **Start**.
2. On **Phone B** (the receiver), connect to Phone A's hotspot network.
3. Go to Phone B's Wi-Fi advanced settings, change Proxy to **Manual**.
4. Enter the **IP Address** displayed on the Sodium app screen.
5. Enter the Port number: `8080`.

### 2️⃣ Method Two: SOCKS5 Configuration (Supports UDP)
*This method requires a client app on the receiving device (like NekoBox, SocksDroid, or any client supporting SOCKS config).*

1. Connect **Phone B** to **Phone A's** hotspot.
2. Open your SOCKS client app on Phone B and add a new profile.
3. Input the **IP Address** generated by the Sodium app on Phone A.
4. Set the standard Port to: `1080`.
5. Tap **Connect** inside the client app to route your traffic without changing system network settings.
